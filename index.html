New Mexico is a state located in the southwestern United States. It is bordered by Colorado to the north, Texas to the east, Oklahoma to the northeast, Arizona to the west, and Mexico to the south. The state has a diverse landscape, with mountains, forests, deserts, and grasslands. The capital of New Mexico is Santa Fe, and the largest city is Albuquerque. The state is known for its rich cultural heritage, with a strong Native American presence and influences from Spanish and Mexican cultures. It is also home to several national parks and monuments, including Carlsbad Caverns National Park and the White Sands National Monument.
